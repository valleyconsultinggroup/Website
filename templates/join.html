Passion for Technology

VCG engages in projects with technology companies of all industries in the Silicon Valley. Projects are carefully evaluated and selected based on their value added to our members. Students from all backgrounds are welcome to join as long as you share an interest in emerging technologies and the modernized world.

No prior business or technical experience is required – all you need is a positive attitude and a willingness to learn!

Career Guidance

VCG strives to ensure the success of all of its members in the professional world. All new members go through an intensive 10-week curriculum which thoroughly prepares them for challenges faced not only as a strategy consultant, but also as a leader in the workplace. VCG members hail prestigious internships, research positions, and full-time jobs at top companies year after year, and we provide all new members with the resources necessary to achieve the same.

Diverse Experience

VCG is a diverse yet close-knit group of individuals that enjoy learning and laughing together. We pride ourselves in creating a comfortable environment where our members have the opportunity to grow both professionally and personally.

VCG holds both formal events like retreats, networking events, resume workshops and more casual events like team dinners, hiking, and cliff diving.
